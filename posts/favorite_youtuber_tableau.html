My Favorite YouTuber to Learn Tableau: sqlbelle

Author: Yichun Chen | Date: October 12, 2023

When it comes to learning Tableau, my favorite resource is sqlbelle, a highly popular YouTuber with 127k subscribers. Her channel is a goldmine for anyone looking to enhance their Tableau skills, whether you're just starting or are more advanced in your data visualization journey.

What makes sqlbelle stand out is her clear and engaging teaching style. She breaks down complex concepts into simple, digestible steps, making it easy for viewers to follow along and apply what they’ve learned. Whether it’s creating interactive dashboards, mastering calculated fields, or using Tableau’s latest features, her tutorials are incredibly informative and approachable.

Some of the topics she covers include:

  • Creating powerful Tableau dashboards
  • Calculated fields for advanced data calculations
  • Using parameter actions to make dashboards more dynamic
  • Understanding LOD (Level of Detail) expressions
  • Data storytelling and visual best practices

What I love most about her channel is how practical her examples are. She frequently demonstrates how Tableau can be applied to real-world scenarios, which makes the learning experience so much more relatable and impactful.

For example, if you’re looking to learn about Level of Detail (LOD) expressions, check out her in-depth video on LODs here. It’s a fantastic resource for anyone looking to gain more control over their data and create more precise calculations in Tableau.

If you’re interested in improving your Tableau skills, I highly recommend checking out sqlbelle’s channel. Her videos have been invaluable to my learning process, and I’m sure they’ll help you too!
